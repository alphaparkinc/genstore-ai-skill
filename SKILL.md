# Genstore AI — AI-Powered E-Commerce Store Builder

Genstore is an AI-native e-commerce platform that lets anyone launch a full online store in **under 2 minutes** using natural language prompts. A built-in team of specialized AI Agents handles everything — design, product sourcing, copywriting, marketing, and analytics — so founders focus on vision, not busywork.

## When to Use

- Building or redesigning an online store without coding or design skills
- Setting up dropshipping or print-on-demand (POD) product catalogs
- Connecting a social media audience (TikTok, Instagram) directly to a storefront
- Writing SEO-optimized product descriptions at scale with AI
- Running marketing campaigns or analyzing store performance via AI chat
- Migrating from Shopify, Amazon, or other platforms to Genstore

---

## Core AI Agents

Genstore ships a team of specialized AI Agents. Invoke each by chatting in plain language:

| Agent | Role | Example Prompt |
|-------|------|----------------|
| **Dean (Design Agent)** | Designs storefronts from brand info (logo, colors, niche) | "Create a minimalist fashion store with pastel tones" |
| **Genius (Super Agent)** | General-purpose advisor: trends, pricing, growth strategy | "What products should I add for summer 2025?" |
| **Analytics Agent** | Revenue insights, conversion rates, top-selling products | "Show me what's selling and what to drop" |
| **Copywriting Agent** | SEO product descriptions from images/titles | "Write descriptions for these 20 shirt images" |
| **Marketing Agent** | Audience targeting, discount campaigns, traffic strategies | "Create a flash sale campaign for returning visitors" |

## Workflow: Launch a Store in 2 Minutes

```
1. Describe your store idea  →  "I want a beauty store targeting Gen Z"
2. Dean designs your storefront  →  brand colors, layout, homepage
3. Add products  →  upload photos (existing) or browse dropship catalog
4. AI writes descriptions  →  SEO copy generated from images
5. Set payment method  →  integrated payment solution
6. Go live  →  one-click publish
7. Analyze & iterate  →  chat with Analytics Agent
```

## Gotchas & Best Practices

- **Brand colors first**: Set your logo and brand colors before designing pages — Dean uses them for all generated layouts.
- **Image quality matters**: Higher-res product photos → better AI-generated descriptions.
- **Use Genius for strategy**: Before adding products, ask Genius for niche + trend recommendations.
- **Analytics before discounts**: Always check the Analytics Agent before creating discount campaigns to avoid cutting margins on already-performing products.
- **POD lead times**: Print-on-demand items have longer fulfillment times than standard dropshipping — set accurate shipping expectations.
- **App stacking**: Combine Free Shipping Upsell + Sales Growth Booster + Wishlist for maximum AOV lift.
